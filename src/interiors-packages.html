FAQ's

In the realm of interior design, Ys Interiors employs factory-made materials that can be customized to meet the unique needs and preferences of our clients and their homes. Our offerings encompass a diverse array of choices, styles, and functionalities, ranging from intelligent features to space-saving solutions, all while embracing contemporary aesthetics.

The following are the materials employed by Ys Interiors in their interior design projects:

  • Medium Density Fibreboard (MDF): This single-board material is composed of bonded wood fibers and is known for its versatility.
  • Commercial Plywood: A favored option for modular kitchens, this material undergoes lamination and polishing to achieve a refined surface finish.
  • Boiling Waterproof (BWP): Ideal for wet areas within kitchens, BWP is a water-resistant modular structure that ensures durability.
  • High-Density High Moisture-Resistant (HDMR) board: Crafted from a combination of fiber chips and forest wood waste, this sturdy material is commonly used for kitchen shutters.
  • High-Density Fiberboard (HDF): HDF is employed in areas requiring strength and rigidity, often found in the construction of bedroom furniture such as Murphy beds, mirror pull-outs, and wardrobes with integrated TV units.
  • Metal: Primarily utilized in bedrooms for industrial, rustic, or contemporary designs, Ys Interiors employs metal in the production of rods, railings, claddings, pipes, and support structures.
  • Particle Boards: These lightweight alternatives to plywood are cost-effective and commonly used for wall partitions, false ceilings, shelves, study desks, and cabinets.

In addition to the aforementioned materials, Ys Interiors incorporates various finishes within their kitchen designs. These include:

  • Veneer: A natural wood finish commonly applied to cabinets, doors, and countertops, providing an organic appearance.
  • Laminates: Also known as sun mica, laminates offer exceptional durability and serve as vibrant substitutes for natural wood and veneers.
  • Lacquer: Employing high-quality glass, lacquered finishes offer superior stain resistance and ease of maintenance, albeit at a higher price point.
  • Acrylic: Offering a similar high-gloss appearance to lacquered glass, acrylic finishes present an affordable alternative, although they are susceptible to scratching.

Ys Interiors collaborates with renowned national and international brands such as Hettich, Grass, Merino, St. Gobin, Asian Paints, and Greenlam. This enables us to directly source a wide range of materials, finishes, paints, wallpapers, upholstery, and fittings for our clients' home interiors. Additionally, we procure popular materials like granite, marble, and quartz from these brands, ensuring exceptional quality and design.

At Ys Interiors, our commitment is to provide the finest materials, finishes, and designs for home interiors. We constantly stay attuned to industry trends to offer our clients the latest innovations in materials and finishes. With our innovative range of interior design materials, we strive to deliver homes that exude exclusivity, comfort, aesthetics, and functionality all in one package.
